Key reminders:
- Literary Magazine editorial, not Wix theme. Think *Harper's*, *The Paris Review*.
- No shadows, no rounded corners, no gradients, no icons.
- Book + journal titles in italic, not quotes.
- Praise blurbs *before* back-cover copy on the book page.
- Sage (`#6B7A5A`) is the one accent; oxblood and sky used sparingly.
- Fraunces display / Source Serif 4 body / Inter small-caps labels.
