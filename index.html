About the Book

Sky Watch

Chasing an American Saddlebred Story

In the 1980s, the American Saddlebred Sky Watch filled more arena seats than any other performance horse, dominating the sport with the power and intensity of a Kentucky Derby winner — earning four World Grand Championships and twelve World titles overall. Sky Watch is not only the tale of a remarkable horse, but of the American Saddlebred breed and the way these horses carried one rider back to herself.

A deeply personal homage to one of the sport's greatest show horses. A Foreword Indies Book of the Year Finalist, praised alongside Susan Orlean and Laura Hillenbrand.

University Press of Kentucky · March 2024 · 280 pp.
